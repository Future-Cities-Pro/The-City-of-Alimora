SafeDose Development Journey

Problem Identification

Recognized that many people forget to take medicine, especially older adults and those with memory issues.

Design Phase

Developed S.A.F.E. principles: Scheduled, Accessible, Flashing lights & sound, Easy to use.

Eco-Friendly Prototype

Built prototype using recycled materials: plastic containers, LEDs, vibration motors, and batteries.

LED Implementation

Installed bright LEDs above pill compartments, visible from up to 20 feet away.

Sound System

Added loud buzzer system for audio alerts calibrated for various hearing levels.

Vibration & Strap

Integrated vibration motor with tactile strap for deaf and hard-of-hearing users.

Power System

Developed battery system using up to 4 "C" batteries with optimized power management.

Multi-Dose Scheduling

Programmed system to support multiple pill times with continuous reminders until confirmed.

User Testing

Conducted testing with older adults and people with hearing impairments for feedback.

App Development

Developed optional smartphone app with Bluetooth connectivity and dose tracking features.

Display Prototype

Assembled functional prototype within 15" deep × 30" wide × 48" high dimensions.

Market Launch

SafeDose ready for market with vision to help millions take medication safely.
